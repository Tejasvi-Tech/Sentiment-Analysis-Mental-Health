
Dataset Name: Mental Health Social Media Dataset  
Source: Kaggle (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/infamouscoder/mental-health-social-media)  
Description:  
- Contains 20,000 labeled English tweets.  
- Labels: Depressed (1) and Non-Depressed (0).  
- Can be used for sentiment analysis and mental health monitoring.  

Notes:  
- Preprocessing required (text cleaning, stopword removal, lemmatization).  
- Data may contain noise due to informal language in tweets.
